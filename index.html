Our First talk

The start of forever

Our first deep talk happened during SCUAA 2025 at MMSU. I remember I had a bad cough then, and out of nowhere, you handed me some medicine. You also made me eat spicy food, even though di ako mahilig sa maanghang. The sunflower seeds will tell the rest of the story... and, of course, yung tumbler ko.

My Dearest Love,

On this special day, I want you to know how incredibly grateful I am for your presence in my life. You are not just my dino pot but my bestfriend, my partner(in crime as well), and my greatest adventure.
